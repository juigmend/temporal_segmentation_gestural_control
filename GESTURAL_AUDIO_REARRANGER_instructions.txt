GESTURAL AUDIO REARRANGER 0.1

DEPENDENCIES:

- Pure Data Extended 0.42.5

- Wekinator 2.1.0.4

- Thalmic Myo armband

- DaemonMYO

OPERATION INSTRUCTIONS:

1) With Pure Data closed, start Myo Connect and then DaemonMYO.

2) Open or make a Wekinator DTW project with 3 inputs and at least 2 outputs (i.e., patterns, gestures).

3) Start Pure Data allowing network connections, then open the patch “GESTURAL_AUDIO_REARRANGER_0.1”. 

4) In Wekinator, click “Start listening”.

5) Click the buttons on the patch and move the Myo.
